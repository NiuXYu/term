PetMinder Privacy Policy

I. Privacy Policy

1.1 Introduction and Overview

PetMinder Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

PetMinder (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy applies to your use of the PetMinder mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We understand the importance you attach to your pet’s health information and promise to collect, use, and store your data only to the extent necessary, and strictly comply with the requirements of the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other relevant laws and regulations.

This Privacy Policy will detail the following for you:

• What types of data we collect

• How we use this data

• Data storage methods

• Your data rights

• Data security measures

• Policy update mechanism

Important Note: This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age and does not contain any child-related functions.

1.2 Data Collection and Use

1.2.1 Types of Data Collected

We collect the following three categories of core data:

(1) Pet Information

• Basic pet information: Name, breed, age, gender, weight, spay/neuter status

(2) Medication Records

• Medication information: Drug name, strength, dosage, quantity, frequency

• Medication details: Medication time (accurate to day/hour)

(3) Pet Avatar Images Uploaded by Users

• Images you select for your pet’s avatar (stored locally on your device)

• Images will only be used to display your pet’s identity within the App and will not be used for any other purposes

1.2.2 Methods of Data Collection

All data in this App adopts a local storage method, does not involve any cloud storage, and all data is only retained on your personal device:

• Pet avatar images: Stored locally on your device and will not be uploaded to any cloud server

• Pet information, basic medication reminder settings, and medication records: All stored locally on your device and only accessible by you on your personal device

• Account registration information: Only stored locally on your device, used for you to log in and use the App on that device, and no cloud synchronization is performed

1.2.3 Purposes of Data Use

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

• Providing core functions: Providing you with medication reminder services and managing your pet’s health records

• Personalized services: Providing customized health advice based on your pet’s breed, age, etc.

• Service improvement: Analyzing local usage patterns (without collecting any user identity-related data) to optimize App functions and user experience

• Customer support: Handling your inquiries and technical support requests (if relevant data is required, it will be proactively submitted by you)

1.3 Data Storage and Security

1.3.1 Storage Architecture

This App adopts a pure local storage architecture, does not set any cloud storage modules, and all data is only stored on your personal device:

• All data (including pet avatars, pet information, medication records, and account registration information) is only stored locally on the user’s device and will not be uploaded to any cloud server, ensuring that data is fully under your control

• Data access rights are only open to you personally, and only you can view, edit, and delete relevant data on your own device

Note: This App does not use any cloud service providers, all data processing is completed on your local device, and there is no need to sign a cloud data processing agreement.

1.3.2 Security Measures

We have implemented strict technical and management measures to protect the security of local data on your device:

Technical Measures:

• Local data encryption: Adopting the AES-256 algorithm to encrypt all data stored on the device at rest, preventing data leakage if the device is lost or illegally accessed

• Access control: Password or biometric authentication (such as fingerprint, facial recognition) access rights can be set within the App to further protect data security

• Data integrity: Using algorithms such as SHA-256 to verify the integrity of local data and prevent data tampering

• Permission control: Only applying for the device permissions necessary for the use of this App (such as storage permission), not obtaining irrelevant permissions, and reducing data security risks

Management Measures:

• Employee training: All employees involved in App development and maintenance have received privacy protection and data security training, and are explicitly prohibited from accessing user local data

• No data access rights: Our staff cannot access, view, or obtain any local data stored on the user’s device

• Regular security assessments: Conducting regular security audits and vulnerability scans on the App to optimize local data encryption and security protection mechanisms

• Incident response: Establishing a complete security incident response mechanism; if a security vulnerability is found in the App, an update will be released to fix it as soon as possible to ensure the security of user local data

1.4 Your Data Rights

In accordance with relevant laws such as GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights:

1.4.1 Right of Access

You have the right to access all personal data and pet-related data collected and stored on your device by us at any time. You can exercise this right in the following ways:

• After logging into the App, directly view all data in the account settings or corresponding data pages

• Contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) via email: driverpmolerh@gmail.com for inquiries about data access

• We will confirm receipt within 10 days of receiving your request and provide a relevant consultation response within 45 days (since data is stored locally on your device, you can directly view the complete data in the App)

1.4.2 Right to Rectification

If you find that the personal data or pet data stored on your device is incorrect, you have the right to request correction. You can exercise this right in the following ways:

• Directly edit the incorrect information in the App, and the modification will be saved to the local device immediately

• Notify us via email, and we will provide corresponding operation guidance

1.4.3 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

In accordance with Article 17 of GDPR, you have the "right to be forgotten". You can delete all your data in the following ways (all operations are completed locally and cannot be recovered after deletion):

(1) Delete Account

• Operation path: In the App, go to "Me" → "User Details" → "Delete Account"

• Confirmation mechanism: Secondary confirmation is required before deletion to prevent accidental deletion

• Deletion scope: Including all personal data, pet information, medication records, account information, etc., which will be completely deleted from your device and cannot be recovered

• Deletion time: After you complete the deletion operation, the data will be deleted from the device immediately, and we will not retain any copies

(2) Delete Specific Data

• You can choose to delete specific pet data or medication records

• Operation path: Long-press the corresponding record on the relevant data page to delete it, and the deletion will take effect immediately

Important Notes:

• Deleting the account and data is an irreversible operation; no data can be recovered after deletion

• We will not retain any identifiable personal information of the deleted data, nor can we recover the deleted data on your device

• However, in accordance with legal requirements, we may need to retain certain anonymized statistical data that does not involve any user identity (only used for internal analysis and not associated with any personal or pet information)

1.4.4 Right to Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data and pet data in a structured, commonly used format (such as CSV, JSON). You can exercise this right in the following ways:

• In the corresponding data export function in the App, directly export local data to your device storage

• Request operation guidance related to data export from us via email

1.4.5 Right to Restriction of Processing

In certain circumstances, you have the right to request us to restrict data processing, for example:

• When there is a dispute about the accuracy of the data

• When the legality of data processing is questioned

• When you have requested data deletion but we need to verify legal requirements

1.4.6 Right to Object

You have the right to object to our data processing based on "legitimate interests" at any time. You can exercise this right in the following ways:

• Change the privacy settings in the App to restrict relevant data processing functions

• Notify us via email, and we will respond to your request in a timely manner

1.5 Data Retention Period

We will retain your local data in accordance with the following principles:

• During the account existence period: When your account is active, the data will be continuously stored locally on your device for your access when using the App

• After account deletion: After you delete your account, all relevant data will be completely deleted from your device immediately, and we will not retain any copies

• Legal requirements: If the law requires us to retain certain data (such as tax records), we will only retain anonymized data that does not involve user identity in accordance with the period specified by law

1.6 Data Sharing and Disclosure

Important Statement: We will not share, rent, or sell any of your data to any third party, and since all data is stored locally on your device, we cannot access or disclose any of your data to third parties.

We may only involve data-related disclosure in the following circumstances (no actual data transmission is involved, only legal explanation):

• Legal requirements: Provide relevant data explanations in accordance with applicable laws, court orders, or requests from law enforcement agencies (since data is stored locally on your device, we will guide you to handle it in accordance with legal requirements)

• Protection of rights: Provide necessary information explanations within the scope permitted by law to protect our rights, property, or user safety

• Business transfer: In the event of company merger, acquisition, or asset sale, we will clearly inform the transferee of the App’s pure local storage model; the transferee has no right to access any user local data and must continue to comply with all terms of this Privacy Policy

1.7 Third-Party Service Description

Important Note: This App does not integrate any third-party partners, advertising services, or cloud services; all functions run on the local device and do not interact with any third-party servers for data.

All data processing of this App is completed on your local device, and no third party is involved in data collection, storage, or processing, ensuring the security and privacy protection of your data.

1.8 Ways to Exercise Your Data Rights

You can exercise your data rights in the following ways:

• In-App operation: Directly view, edit, delete, and export your local data through functions such as account settings and data management in the App

• Email: Send to driverpmolerh@gmail.com to consult relevant questions or submit requests to exercise rights

We will respond to your request as soon as possible after receiving it. In accordance with legal requirements, we will confirm receipt of your request within 10 days and complete the processing within 45 days.
